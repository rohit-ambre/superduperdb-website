AI in your database

A unifying open-source environment for developers that enables you to effortlessly integrate, and unleash AI directly within your database - from LLMs, public APIs to custom high-performance in-house models.

For developers, data scientists and engineers alike

Level up your application with AI by integrating it directly into your database

  • Deploy any PyTorch model in your database with a single Python command.
  • Train models with no overhead, simply by querying the training data from your database.
  • Integrate APIs such as OpenAI to work together with your data and other models.
  • Model outputs are stored alongside their inputs in the database, instantly available to all teams.
