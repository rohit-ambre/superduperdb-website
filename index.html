About Us

An AI-database solution revolutionizing how AI models and data work together.

AI database

By integrating your deep learning models directly into your database, you'll win never seen before transparency, auditability, agility, and ease of productionization.

A web-scale customizable and managed semantic search solution in one line of code? No problem!

A real time intelligent insights monitoring solution on financial data? No problem!

SuperDuperDB allows users to define deep-learning models which listen directly to inserts, updates and deletes, and which dynamically update model outputs saved in collection documents. That means production-izable retrieval, classification, retrieval augmented language modelling, multi-modal learning and much, much more, can be just a few lines of vanilla PyTorch code away.
